This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of individuals using our Provincial Budget & Financial Management Information System ("PBFMIS"). This policy is compliant with relevant laws and data protection regulations of the Republic of the Philippines, including the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name
  • Office or Department
  • Position or Designation

This information is collected solely for internal and official use within the provincial government.

2. Purpose of Collection

  • To authenticate and identify users within the system
  • To associate financial transactions and documents with responsible officers
  • To ensure audit, traceability, and accountability in financial operations

3. Legal Basis

  • Mandate of the Provincial Government to maintain accurate records
  • Section 12 of RA 10173, allowing data processing for public interest

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your information will not be shared with third parties outside the provincial government, unless:

  • Required by law
  • With your explicit consent
  • For lawful government audits or investigations

5. Data Protection and Security

We apply appropriate organizational, physical, and technical measures to protect your personal data against:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Loss or theft
  • Use or disclosure
  • Copying or modification

System access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the stated purposes or as required by government retention policies.

7. Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Be informed about data processing
  • Access your personal data
  • Object to or withdraw consent
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC)

To exercise these rights, contact us below.
